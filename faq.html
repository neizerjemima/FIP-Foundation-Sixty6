Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Foundation Sixty6?


    • Foundation Sixty6 is formed by the parents and supporters of a child who has experienced significant barriers in accessing adequate mental healthcare for severe conditions that have recently been diagnosed under the umbrella of psychotic depression.

  • What is the mission of Foundation Sixty6?


    • The mission is To facilitate improved access to mental healthcare for youth between the ages of 16 and 25 suffering from psychosis, bipolar disorder (BPD), and schizophrenia, specifically within Southwestern Ontario. We aim to achieve this by creating a network of physicians and mental healthcare providers who will offer counselling services for free, or at a reduced rate, and by generating donations / funds to support that access. We also aim to create long term employment opportunities that understand the flexibility and support required by people with severe mental health challenges.

  • How can I get involved as a volunteer with Foundation Sixty6?


    • To become a volunteer with Foundation Sixty6, you'll need to complete the volunteer application form found on their website. Once you've submitted your application, you can proceed with the steps to inquire about opportunities: attend orientation or training, and get involved in their initiatives. Keep in touch with the organization's volunteer coordinator or relevant staff members to stay updated and engaged in your volunteering journey.

  • What are the requirements or qualifications for volunteering with Foundation Sixty6?


    • The requirements or qualifications for volunteering with Foundation Sixty6 are not highly specific. Essentially, anyone can be a part of our volunteer team. However, we highly value individuals, especially professionals in the healthcare field, who can provide guidance and support to the young individuals experiencing mental health disorders. While there are no strict prerequisites, having expertise or experience in mental health care can greatly enhance the impact of your volunteer contributions. We welcome all who are passionate about making a difference in the lives of those affected by mental health challenges.

  • How can I donate to support the work of Foundation Sixty6?


    • To donate and support the work of Foundation Sixty6, simply visit our website. There, you'll find the "Donate" button, which will direct you to our secure donation platform. Once there, you can choose the amount you wish to contribute and select your preferred payment method. Your donation will directly support our programs and initiatives aimed at helping individuals overcome mental health challenges and build better futures. We greatly appreciate your generosity and commitment to our cause.

  • What impact will my donation have on the programs and services of Foundation Sixty6?


    • Your donation will directly fund programs that provide essential support and resources to individuals struggling with mental health challenges. This could include access to therapy, counseling services, support groups, and educational workshops. With increased funding, Foundation Sixty6 can expand its reach and offer its services to more people in need. This could involve opening new counseling centers, establishing outreach programs in underserved communities, or developing innovative initiatives to address specific mental health issues. Your donation enables Foundation Sixty6 to invest in training and professional development for staff and volunteers, ensuring that they can provide the highest quality of care and support to those they serve. Overall, your donation plays a crucial role in enabling Foundation 66 to fulfill its mission of supporting individuals with mental health challenges and fostering healthier, happier communities.

  • Are there any fundraising events or campaigns that I can participate in to support Foundation Sixty6?


    • * We organize awareness walks or runs to raise funds and promote mental health awareness. You can participate by registering for the event, forming a team, or seeking sponsors to donate on your behalf.
    * We may host galas or benefit dinners where attendees can purchase tickets to enjoy a night of entertainment while supporting the organization. You can attend these events, invite friends and family, or even contribute by donating auction items or sponsoring tables.
    * We may launch online crowdfunding campaigns through platforms like GoFundMe or Kickstarter. You can donate directly to these campaigns or help spread the word by sharing them on social media and encouraging others to contribute.
    By participating in these fundraising events or campaigns, you can actively contribute to Foundation Sixty6's mission of supporting individuals with mental health challenges and promoting mental wellness in your community. Keep an eye on their website, social media channels, or email newsletters for updates on upcoming events and ways to get involved.

  • How can I stay informed about the work and impact of Foundation Sixty6?


    • 1. Visit our website for updates.
    2. Sign up for our email newsletter.
    3. Follow us on social media.
    4. Attend our events.
    5. Volunteer with us for regular updates.
