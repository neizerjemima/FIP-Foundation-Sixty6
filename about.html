The parents and supporters of a child who has experienced significant barriers in accessing adequate mental healthcare for severe conditions that have recently been diagnosed under the umbrella of psychotic depression.

Where Are We Now?

• We have established a corporation, Blue Sunflower Corporation (BSCI), to drive the commercial aspects of this initiative.

• Foundation Sixty6 has been officially formed.

• Domain names have been officially registered and obtained for both legal entities.

• We have started identifying and adding HCPs to the list of professionals willing to support this initiative. This list is growing daily.

• We have begun to identify potential corporate sponsors for future fundraising initiatives.

• We have formed an alliance with Fanshawe through the SILEx Program
